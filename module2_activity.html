About Bataan

Bataan is a province located in the Central Luzon region of the Philippines. It is known for its rich history, beautiful landscapes, and cultural heritage.

Tourist Attraction

  • Mount Samat National Shrine
  • Las Casas Filipinas de Acuzar
  • Bataan National Park

History

Bataan played a significant role during World War II, particularly during the Battle of Bataan and the subsequent Bataan Death March. The province is rich in historical landmarks and memorials.
